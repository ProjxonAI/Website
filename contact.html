Get in touch

We're just an email away

Projxon is still in pre-launch mode, so the best way to reach us is the old-fashioned way: send a note to support@projxon.ai.

Primary inbox

Email support@projxon.ai for everything— partnerships, product questions, press, or just to say hi.

What to include

A few sentences about who you are, how you'd like to use Projxon, and anything we should know helps us route your note faster.

What happens after you email us?

We're a lean team, but we read every message. Here's what to expect.

1. Quick acknowledgement

You'll get a confirmation within 1 business day so you know we received your note.

2. Thoughtful reply

A human (likely a founder) replies within 3 days with next steps or questions.

3. Follow-up

If it makes sense, we'll jump on a call or invite you into the private beta.

We'd love to hear from you

Tell us about how you're thinking about local AI, what you're building, or why Projxon resonates. Every email informs the product.
