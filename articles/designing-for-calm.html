Designing for Calm Interfaces

Nov 2, 2025 4 min read By Elena Ruiz

Principles we lean on

  1. Quiet hierarchy — We keep typography intentional and let gradients do the heavy lifting.
  2. Soft motion — Animations signal context changes, never urgency.
  3. Purposeful contrast — Dark shells with focused accents mirror our local-first mindset.

Collaboration loops

Every week we run crit with engineering and research at the same table. It keeps decisions aligned and prevents the "handoff" gaps that often derail early teams.
