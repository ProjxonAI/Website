Building Local AI with Intention

Nov 9, 2025 5 min read By Maya Chen

Why local matters

The closer intelligence lives to the person using it, the less it has to borrow context or leak information. We start every prototype by asking what needs to stay on device, not what can we cache in the cloud.

  • Local-first keeps latency down.
  • Local-first makes trust tangible.
  • Local-first encourages better product decisions.

Craft over hype

We're intentionally shipping small surfaces first. That means tight instrumentation, lots of manual onboarding, and an obsessive focus on how calm the product feels day to day.
